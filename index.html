The Highline Trail, Glacier National Park

About the Trail

Length: 11.4 miles

Trailhead: Located across Going-to-the-Sun Road from the Logan Pass Visitor Center parking lot, or at the Loop.

Experience the spectacular beauty of Glacier on this hike that parallels the Garden Wall. Gradually climb up to the junction for Grinnell Glacier Overlook then continue to Granite Park Chalet. The remaining four miles are a steep 2,200 foot descent to the Loop. The free Going-to-the-Sun shuttle is available for transportation between Logan pass and the Loop.
